Forum Mall

Location: Chennai, Bangalore, Hyderabad (India)

Forum Mall is a popular shopping mall that offers a wide range of shops, restaurants, and entertainment options. It is developed by the Prestige Group.

Features:

  • Clothing and lifestyle stores
  • Food court and restaurants
  • Movie theatre (PVR)
  • Kids' play area
  • Parking space

Timings:

10:00 AM to 10:00 PM
